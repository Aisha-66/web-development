Asian Sesame Dressing

Ingredients

2 tbsp light soy sauce (Note 1)

1 tbsp sesame oil (toasted)

2 tbsp grapeseed oil

3 tbsp rice vinegar

1 tsp sugar

1 garlic clove

1/2 tsp black pepper

1 1/2 tsp fresh ginger

SALAD

4 cups Chinese cabbage

1 1/2 cups red cabbage

1 cup carrot

2 cups chicken

1/2 cup shallots / scallions

GARNISHES

  • 1/2 to 1 cup crunchy noodles

Instructions

Combine the Dressing ingredients in a jar and shake. Set aside for 10 minutes or so for the flavours to meld. Place the Salad ingredients together in a large bowl along with half the crunchy noodles. Drizzle over dressing then toss. (Note 4) Divide between serving bowls. Top with more crunchy noodles and a good sprinkle of sesame seeds. Serve immediately!
